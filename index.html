This Privacy Policy explains how our application Mutauro wangu collects, uses, and protects information. The App is designed for educational purposes and may be used by children under 13. We comply with Google Play’s Families Policy Requirements and ensure high standards of privacy and safety.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without parental consent.

2. Information We Collect

a. Information You Provide

The App does not require users to create an account and does not ask for personal information such as name, email, phone number, or address.

b. Automatically Collected Information

The App may collect limited, non-personal technical data to improve functionality, including:

  • Device type and model

  • App crash logs

This information cannot identify a specific user.

c. Children's Data

The App does not collect, store, or request any personal information from children. No location data, photos, contacts, or audio are accessed.

Any collected non-personal data is used only to:

  • Understand general usage patterns

4. Third-Party Services

The App may use the following services:

a. Google Play Services

Used for essential app functionality and stability reporting.

b. Firebase Analytics / Crashlytics (if used)

If enabled, these tools collect anonymous analytics and crash data. They do not collect personally identifiable information.

c. Advertising

The App does not display targeted advertising to children. If ads are added in the future, only Google-approved, family-safe ads will be used.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or disclose user information to third parties except when:

We implement administrative, technical, and physical measures to protect all collected data. Because only non-personal information is collected, risk is minimized—especially for children.
